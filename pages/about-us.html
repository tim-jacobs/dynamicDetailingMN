About Us

Our Story

Born and raised in St. Joseph, Minnesota, we have always been very driven and motivated towards creating our own business. At the age of fifteen, we tested a few different business ventures, none of which ignited true passion. At the age of seventeen, we decided to get into detailing. We started Dynamic Detailing because we are perfectionists. We take pride in the things we do, and enjoy making others happy and stress free with our hard work and attention to detail. We first began providing our services to friends and family, and later expanded our business to others who sought for our pristine work.

Over the years of investing into our company, learning the best techniques, and pleasing our greatly valued clients, Dynamic Detailing has transformed into what it is today.
